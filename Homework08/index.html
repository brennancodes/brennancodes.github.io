About Me

Hello there! My name is Brennan Spicer. I am a web developer hailing from the great state of Minnesota, raised in the even greater city of Eagan. I'm a 25 year old college graduate currently living with three longtime friends and our house mascot, Sawyer, the border collie.

Web development has interested me for a long time, but I only recently began pursuing it as a career. I began my studies independently and elected to continue them with help from the best at the University of Minnesota's Coding Boot Camp. I've learned a lot already but there's still plenty more out there!

In my free time I enjoy nerding out to all things Star Wars, Lord of the Rings, Game of Thrones, video games, and more. I also take pleasure in writing and playing inordinate amounts of subpar music! If I'm not at home or at work, you can find me studying in coffee shops, hiking around anything that slightly resembles wilderness, or enjoying a night out with my closest friends.

I'm a tenacious person who takes pride in the work that I do. I will work tirelessly to secure the confidence of those around me and submit work we can all be proud of. I'm always grateful and excited for new opportunities to learn and grow as a developer and a person, and I'm eager to see what awaits me in this beautiful life.

While I'm out on my adventures, please enjoy browsing my portfolio to see what I've been up to lately, and don't hesitate to reach out to me via the Contact page. I'm always happy to build my network with new friends and business partners!
